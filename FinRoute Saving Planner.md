Privacy Policy
FinRoute Saving Planner
Last updated: January 2025
FinRoute Saving Planner (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.
 
Information Collection and Use
FinRoute Saving Planner does not collect, track, or share any personal data.
The App:
•	Does not use advertising services
•	Does not use analytics or tracking technologies
•	Does not collect identifiers such as Device ID or Advertising ID
•	Does not track users across apps or websites
 
Data Storage
Any data you create in the App (such as savings goals or plans) is:
•	Stored locally on your device and/or
•	Securely stored using Apple CloudKit, which is managed entirely by Apple
FinRoute Saving Planner does not have access to your Apple ID or personal iCloud information.
 
Data Sharing
FinRoute Saving Planner does not share data with third parties.
 
Children’s Privacy
FinRoute Saving Planner does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
 
Security
The App relies on Apple’s built-in security and encryption technologies to protect data handled by the operating system and iCloud services.
 
Changes to This Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
